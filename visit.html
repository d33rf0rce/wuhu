Visa and Immigration

Wuhu Island has the following categories for nationals of foreign countries and territories to visit, travel or work on the islands.

These are split in the following lists:

  • Annex I: Foreign nationals eligible to travel, live and work
  • Annex II: Foreign Nationals eligible to visa-free travel
  • Annex III: All other regions require visas

Annex I

  • State of Almondria: Passport, National ID

Annex II

  • United States of America
  • European Union
  • Japan
  • Canada
